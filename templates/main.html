Male Vaccination

As the vaccination drive picks up pace in India, the gender gap in vaccination rate (people vaccinated per 1,000 individuals) continues to widen. According to our analysed data, almost 25% males have got the first dose.

Female Vaccination

Transgender Vaccination

Vaccination Per State(1st dose)

According to our analysed data, 5 states have vaccinated more than 50% of its population.Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu is on the top with almost 66% of its population vaccinated while Uttar Pradesh is on the tail with almost 11%.

Vaccination Per State(2nd dose)

According to our analysed data, 3 states have vaccinated more than 10% of its population with 2nd dose. Tripura is on the top with almost 15% of its population vaccinated with 2nd dose while Bihar is on the tail with almost 2%.

Covishield Doses

According to our analysed data, 12 states have used 100% of its vaccines as Covishield while Delhi has done the lowest use of Covishield with almost 75% of its total doses.

Covaxin Doses

According to our analysed data, 12 states have not used Covaxin vaccines while Delhi has done the highest use of Covaxin with almost 25% of its total doses.
